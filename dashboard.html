About

Femi Aluko is the co-founder and CEO of Chowdeck, raised in Akesan, Alimosho LGA, Lagos State. He studied Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Obafemi Awolowo University, graduating as the best student in the Telecommunications option. Before founding Chowdeck, he was one of Paystack's earliest engineers — joining as the fifth engineer and first Core Payments Engineer in 2017 — eventually rising to Principal Engineer before departing in 2021.

Chowdeck is a Nigerian on-demand food delivery and restaurant logistics platform founded in October 2021, headquartered in Lagos. The platform connects over 1 million registered users with restaurants across 8 Nigerian cities, processing approximately ₦30 billion worth of deliveries in 2024. In June 2025, Chowdeck acquired Mira, a POS startup serving 500+ restaurants, signalling expansion into restaurant infrastructure.

Why Builder Series

Femi is a deeply technical founder who went from architecting payment infrastructure at one of Africa's most celebrated fintechs to building what is becoming Nigeria's leading food-delivery platform — bringing an engineer's rigour to a messy, last-mile logistics problem. His trajectory from a modest upbringing in Alimosho to YC-backed founder and Future Awards Africa 2024 winner is a compelling Lagos origin story. Chowdeck is a high-visibility consumer product Lagos audiences use daily, making Femi instantly relatable to Builder Series viewers.

  1. 1You grew up in Akesan, Alimosho — not the part of Lagos that usually makes it into startup origin stories. How did that environment shape the way you think about building products for everyday Nigerians?

About

Rank (formerly Moni Africa) is a Lagos-based fintech startup that provides group financing and working capital loans to small businesses and mobile money agents across Africa. The company leverages social trust and collective group responsibility as collateral, replacing traditional credit scoring. With a reported ~99% repayment rate, Rank targets Africa's 44+ million underserved SMBs.

Adedapo ('Dapo') Sobayo is co-founder and CTO of Rank, a YC W22-backed fintech. He has a background in blockchain development and co-founded Moni in 2021 alongside Femi Iromini, going through Y Combinator's Winter 2022 batch.

Why Builder Series

Dapo is building a fintech solution that turns social trust into a financial product — cracking the SMB credit problem in Africa without traditional collateral. The ~99% repayment rate challenges conventional fintech assumptions. As a YC-backed CTO co-founder serving Africa's 44 million+ small businesses, he represents exactly the technical, mission-driven Lagos founder the Builder Series audience wants to hear from.
